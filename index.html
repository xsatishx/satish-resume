About

I am a Cloud Solutions Architect focusing on cloud infrastructure and application innovation for strategic customers, often across multi-country and multi-year committed deals.

My work involves guiding customers through end-to-end digital transformation journeys, from initial pilots and proofs of concept to scaled production deployments aligned to evolving business needs.

To help customers mitigate risk and accelerate value, I lead architectural design sessions, Cloud Adoption Framework assessments, and Well-Architected Framework reviews.

I frequently speak at customer and partner events and have received the Microsoft Event Speaker Silver Award.

While I have worked across multiple domains, a significant portion of my experience has been in healthcare and financial services.

One of my proudest professional milestones was leading a team at a fast-paced startup from inception to acquisition within four years.

I love helping others grow. I mentor on MentorCruise, where I am rated as one of their top mentors.
